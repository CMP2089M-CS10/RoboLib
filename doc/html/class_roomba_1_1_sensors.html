 The angle that Roomba has turned through since the angle was last requested. The angle is expressed as the difference in the distance traveled by Roombaâ€™s two wheels in millimeters, specifically the right wheel distance minus the left wheel distance, divided by two. This makes counter-clockwise angles positive and clockwise angles negative. This can be used to directly calculate the angle that Roomba has turned through since the last request.
